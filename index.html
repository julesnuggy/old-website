About Me

Hi, I'm Jules and this is my first webpage! Through my career to date, I have become a successful and well-respected management consultant, specialising in innovation, business change and data analytics. In this time, I worked on advice and support to clients in the defence, government, transport, engineering and aerospace industries. After eight years of this, I have decided that I need a change in my career - to finally make the jump and delve into the world of programming and coding. This has long been an interest of mine - my favourite past projects as a management consultant were those that involved building tools and models using VBA for Excel, typically to churn through masses of data to derive meaningful insights and generate visualisations. This coding skill, which I developed from the ground up through self-teaching whilst working, has always fascinated and inspired me. Developing a tangible output which the customer could use over and over again, and the journey of getting there - overcoming many obstacles and learning new things each day - has always been extremely satisfying for me. Hence why I want to join Founders & Coders, so that I can become a fully fledged coder and begin my journey of becoming a professional!
